# Account Deletion - Omnitask

## How to Request Account Deletion

If you want to delete your account and associated data from **Omnitask**, please follow the steps below:

1. Send an email to **amanthapliyal14@gmail.com** from the email address registered with your Omnitask account.
2. Use the subject line: **Account Deletion Request**
3. In the email body, include:
   - Registered email address
   - Username (if applicable)

Once the request is received, our team will verify it and begin the account deletion process.

## Deletion Timeline

- Your Omnitask account and associated personal data will be permanently deleted within **90 days** of receiving the deletion request.

## Data That Will Be Deleted

The following data will be deleted:
- User account information (email, username)
- Authentication data
- App-generated user content associated with the account

## Data That May Be Retained

The following data may be retained for legal, security, or compliance purposes:
- Transaction or payment records (if applicable)
- System logs required for fraud prevention or legal obligations

Any retained data will be securely stored and deleted after the legally required retention period.
